Criteria and Weightages for Admission

Describe each criterion with its respective weightage i.e., Admission Test, marks in qualifying examination, etc.

The Criteria for admission are prescribed by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh and JNTU University, Ananthapuramu issued every year, through a common prospectus for admission of professional degree courses.

Qualification: A pass in 12th Standard of Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate (Academic) with mathematics, physics, and chemistry or equivalent. For SC/ST candidates, a mere pass in the qualifying examination will suffice and the minimum marks are the same as the passing minimum for APSCHE/Diploma of the State of Andhra Pradesh. Candidates belonging to communities other than SC/ST should have obtained the minimum marks for admission.
